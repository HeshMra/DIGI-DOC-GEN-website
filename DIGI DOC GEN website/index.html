Image processing & Natural Language Processing Based Digitalized Document Generator Application

Due to their busy schedules, the majority of people today struggle with day-to-day life. As a result, it takes a lot of time for students and other workers to create reports using word processing software. In order to turn handwriting, sketches, and graphs into fully digital documents, we are proposing a system. In addition, if they lack the time to create the information in a paper, they can easily enter the data by speaking. Using their handwritten notes and speech recognition, users of this system can quickly produce reports. The specialty of this application is it helps blind people to do their documents in a user-friendly manner and in here this application supposed to build with voice commands and they can easily access each function.

Literature Review

Handwriting recognition, also known as optical character recognition (OCR), is an automated process that converts handwritten documents and images into digitized text. There are several existing solutions and research studies for converting handwritten images into digitized documents. A common way to convert handwritten documents into digitized text is to use OCR software. OCR software uses a scanner to capture the physical form of a document and, converts the scanned image into a two-color or black and white version. The software analyzes the light and dark areas of the image and identifies the dark areas as characters to be recognized. However, while OCR technology provides over 99% accuracy on characters typed on high-quality images, various human typefaces, spacing differences, and handwriting irregularities make character recognition of handwritten documents difficult and less accurate. Therefore, in the field of handwriting recognition, research is ongoing to improve the accuracy of character recognition in handwritten documents. Examples of research in this area include software solutions that automatically convert handwritten images to text. Research Problem

The primary research problem addressed in this study is the difficulty faced by individuals in effectively managing their time to complete documents within the given deadlines. This problem is exacerbated by various factors, including the inability to quickly type or write content, create tables, and insert images. Additionally, blind individuals encounter challenges in writing documents within short time periods, particularly when it comes to drawing sketches and creating advanced charts. Another issue is the lack of attention to deadlines due to heavy workloads, resulting in missed deadlines. Furthermore, individuals often forget to include important elements such as references and abbreviations in glossaries. These combined factors contribute to inefficiencies in document completion and negatively impact overall productivity and effectiveness. Therefore, this research aims to explore potential solutions and strategies to help individuals better manage their time and improve the efficiency and quality of document creation processes.

    1. People cannot manage the time properly to complete the documents within the given time properly.

    3. For the blind people who needs to write documents at a given short time period cannot complete because of inability to write and also it is difficult to them to draw sketches and advanced charts by their own in a very short period.
    4. People don not pay much attention to the deadlines of the reports because of the workload and missed the deadlines.
    5. Forget to insert references, abbreviations in glossaries.


Research Objectives

The team came up with four objectives to address the research challenge after thoroughly analyzing the research gap.

  1. Detect the handwriting using image processing and convert it into a digitalized document and detect the deadline of the relevant document
  2. Detect voice recognized text using NLP, check grammar, insert abbreviations and give reference to the sentence.
  3. Identify the relevant images that should insert into the document and insert them using NLP and generate conclusion.
  4. Suggest referenced sentences or paragraphs through websites related to the topic and implement the voice commands within entire application.
